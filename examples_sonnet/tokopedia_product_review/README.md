## Key Findings

1. **Extreme positive skew**: 93.9% of reviews are 5-star ratings
2. **Bucketed sold counts**: 68.1% of sold_count values are round numbers (100, 250, 500, etc.)
3. **Review length inverse to rating**: Lower ratings produce longer reviews
4. **Equitable distribution**: No extreme concentration of reviews on few products
5. **Temporal growth**: Sharp increase in reviews from 2021 onward
6. **High rating uniformity**: 77% of products with 10+ reviews show very low rating variance

## Data Quality Notes

- 59.2% missing product variants
- Perfect sentiment-rating alignment suggests derived labels
- 5.2% of reviews contain <10 characters
- No duplicate reviews found
- Date range: 2015-11-18 to 2025-12-12 (10 years)
